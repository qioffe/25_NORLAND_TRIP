Project Timeline

A comprehensive schedule of key milestones for the visa application process.

  • Week 1: September 3 – 9 Initial preparations
    ▸

    Key Actions:

    SEPTEMBER 3
    Initial student information verification begins.
    SEPTEMBER 4-7
    Communication channels with parents are updated, and the initial project roadmap is announced.
    SEPTEMBER 8-9
    Final student data is compiled, including home addresses and contact details. The official invitation letter is issued.
  • Week 2: September 10 – 15 Visa application process
    ▸

    Key Actions:

    SEPTEMBER 10
    The application is finalized and parents must sign documents, including the pre-filled COVA form. The first payment amount is also announced.
    SEPTEMBER 11
    The complete visa packet is dispatched to Washington, D.C. An air ticket quote is provided to parents.
    SEPTEMBER 15
    The stamped visa passport is collected.
  • Future Payments October & November
    ▸

    Payment Schedule:

    OCTOBER
    The second payment is due.
    NOVEMBER
    The third and final payment is due.
  • Notarized Consent Letter ▸

    A notarized consent letter is required if a parent is not accompanying the minor.

  • Passport & Identification ▸
    • Passport – valid for at least six (6) months, with a minimum of two (2) blank visa pages.
    • Photocopy of passport bio-page.
    • Birth Certificate for minor applicant.
  • COVA Visa Form ▸

    Completed online at COVA Form, printed in full (8–9 pages).

  • Supporting Documentation ▸
    • Photocopy of most recent Chinese visa or residence permit, if applicable.
    • Proof of residence in the United States.
    • Completed “Where You Stay” Form.
  • Invitation Letter ▸

    Includes minor’s full name, purpose of visit, intended dates, places to visit, inviter info, official seal/signature, and date of issuance.
