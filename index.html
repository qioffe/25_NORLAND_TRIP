Complete the Online Application

A parent or guardian must fill out the form online. Follow these required actions:

language

Go to the official Chinese visa website and select your application location (North America -> Washington, D.C.).

open_in_new
edit_document

Fill out the form completely and accurately, following the on-screen prompts.

print

Save your application number and print all pages of the form (typically 8-9 pages).

draw

Sign the printed form. A parent or guardian must sign the confirmation page and Section 9.1. The person who filled out the form must also sign the "Application Preparer" section.

Important: The minor should not sign any part of the application.
